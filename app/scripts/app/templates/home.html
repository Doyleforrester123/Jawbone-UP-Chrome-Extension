Recording tips

You have {{cookiesLength}} cookies saved for this website. Cookies

During replay, all scenarios start with an empty cookie jar. We therefore recommend that you clear the cookies before each recording, so as to avoid any discrepancies between record and replay.

Record the login

Make sure you record the login phase as well so that the scenario can be replayed correctly in BugBuster.

Limitations

The recorder currently does not support recording:
- inside iframes
- drag & drop
To bypass these limitations, edit the recorded script after it is exported.
